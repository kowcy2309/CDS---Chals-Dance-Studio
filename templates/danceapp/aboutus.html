ABOUT US

Chals Dance was founded in 2001 and has been steadily growing ever since. Our studio is involved in various dance-related activities, including choreography for college, corporate events, schools, and media competitions. Some of our notable events include Kerala Mathraboomi Film Fare Award, Vijay Tele Awards, Sun TV Masthana Masthana Dance Reality, Z Telugu Magatheera Dance Reality, ETV Dhee Dance Reality Show, MAA TV Garshana Dance Reality Show, and many more.

The founder, a well-trained choreographer and dancer proficient in various dance forms, has been in the field for over 15 years, choreographing and performing in over 1000 shows. They were also part of the MARGA Dance Company, an international dance company that performed overseas. Additionally, the founder of Pandavas Dance Academy has choreographed and judged numerous dance and fashion shows for corporate companies and the media.
