CHARLES

Artistic director, Sr. Charles, is a highly talented dancer with extensive experience in a variety of dance forms, including Bollywood, Western, Classical, Folk, and Hip-Hop. He founded CDS - CHALS School of Dance in October 2001.

The studio has been successfully running since October 2001 and has grown to become a major dance studio in Chennai, India. Students from diverse backgrounds enroll in CDS to learn dance in its traditional form. CDS's approach of imparting a variety of dances in their pure and traditional form is highly popular and welcomed by all our patrons.
